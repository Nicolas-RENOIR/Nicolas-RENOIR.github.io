Project information

  • Category: Network security
  • Made for: Public entity
  • Project date: January 2023 - June 2023
  • Language: Python

I collaborated with Risk'n tic to design a device capable of scanning, analyzing, and mapping a network. The data collected during this scan was integrated into the Risk'n tic application to perform reliable and rapid risk analyses (EBIOS RM analyses). In order to make this data accessible and usable, I developed a secure web application with a dynamic map displaying business values, IP addresses, operating systems... and a list of vulnerabilities detected on the network, categorized by severity.
